About Vocal Vent Corporate Space:

The Vocal Vent Corporate Space offers company or institution employees/members a 10 Minute Space 4 Times a Month to vent and release whatever is holding them back, Speaking it out is always a step in letting it go.

Companies/Corporates/Institutions sign up and subscribe for their members as a well being program for them and allow a private anonymous avenue to speak their frustrations. A separate service with licensed therapists is also available as a different package.
